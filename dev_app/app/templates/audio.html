Let your cat try out this clumping cat litter which is made from spruce and fir timber from sustainable sources.

The fibres in OkoPlus organic clumping cat litter have a capillary action attracting liquid on contact whilst effectively trapping odours.

This great cat litter contains a natural gelling agent which gives Okoplus its clumping action.

OkoPlus is 100% compostable and bio-degradable.
